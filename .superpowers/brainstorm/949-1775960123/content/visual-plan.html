Condition Cards — Medical Illustrations

Current: Text-only cards with condition name + description. Small emoji-style icons.

Proposed:

  • Add anatomical illustration to each condition card showing the affected area

  • Cards become image-top, text-bottom layout
  • Helps patients visually identify their condition
