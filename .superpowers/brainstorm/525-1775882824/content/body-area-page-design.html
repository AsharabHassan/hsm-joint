Design Decisions Shown Above

  • Page order: Nav → Trust bar → Hero → Quiz → Social proof → Conditions → Stats → Risks → Comparison → Treatments → Testimonials → Location → FAQ
  • Hero: Left-aligned, editorial. No decorative orbs or gradients. Clean negative space. Charcoal CTA button.
  • Quiz: Full-width section right after hero. White card on cream. Charcoal selected state (not gold). Gold gradient progress bar.
  • Typography: Serif headings (editorial feel). Clean sans body. Generous letter-spacing on labels.
  • Color: Cream (#FEFCF8) base, white cards, charcoal CTAs, gold for accents/labels only. No navy backgrounds.
  • Cards: Subtle borders (#F0EBE0), soft shadows. No border-top accents. SVG icons in warm-tinted containers.
  • Section pattern: Gold line divider → Serif heading → Subtitle → Content grid
